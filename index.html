Welcome to Indicus Dreams

We are a non-conformist band, performing across Bengaluru and Chennai, that predominantly delves into fusion music. We specialise in genre-bending, mind-altering music! We also cover Indian Film Music (regional and English), across genres such as Carnatic, Hindustani, Latin Pop, Jazz, and Rock. We experiment with and try to add our own flavors to each song we perform. We write and perform our own songs as well!
